Felipe Rodolfo Hendriksen is a Professor in Humanities graduated at Pontificia Universidad Católica Argentina, with a passion for comics, manga, anime, popular culture, and modern folklore. He has completed two exchange programs in England and the United States, where he honed his English and Spanish skills and gained valuable teaching and writing experience.

Currently, he is writing his thesis on Borges and heroism under the supervision of Dr. Javier Roberto González, while also working as an adscripto a las cátedras de Literatura Española Medieval I y II at UCA. He has contributed to various literary publications as an editor, columnist, and writer, and has corrected texts for a testimonial book. He has multiple certifications and honors in English and academic performance and is interested in pursuing further resech and education in his field.
